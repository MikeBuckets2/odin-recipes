Chicken and Sausage Gumbo Recipe

Cajun Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

A blend of chicken, smoky sausage and a rich, dark roux.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lb chicken thighs or breasts, diced
  • 1 lb smoked sausage, sliced
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooked white rice
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Make a dark roux by whisking flour and oil over medium heat until deep brown.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, and celery; cook until soft.
  3. Stir in garlic, sausage, and chicken; cook until browned.
  4. Pour in stock, add seasonings, and simmer 45 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice and garnish with green onions.

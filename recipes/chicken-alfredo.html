Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Classic Chicken Alfredo

Slices of grilled chicken breast served over a bed of fettucine pasta, all coated in an alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 oz fettucine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Boil fettucine in salted water until al dente; drain and set aside
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, then cook in oil or butter unitl golden; slice
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and saute garlic until fragrant
  4. Stir in cream and Parmesan; simmer until sauce thickens
  5. Toss pasta in sauce, add sliced chicken, and mix well
  6. Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan; serve warm

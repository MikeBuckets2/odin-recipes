Shrimp Etouffee Recipe

Classic Louisiana Shrimp Etouffee

Tender shrimp added to sauce where they are smothered in gravy served over rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 1/2 cups seafood stock or water
  • 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooked white rice
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet; add onion, bell pepper, and celery, cooking until soft
  2. Stir in garlic and flour; cook 2 minutes to form a light roux
  3. Gradually whisk in stock, then add Cajun seasoning, paprika, salt, and pepper
  4. Add shrimp; simmer 5-7 minutes until pink and sauce thickens
  5. Serve over rice and garnish with green onions
